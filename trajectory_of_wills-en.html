Trajectory of Wills

2012-2014

Video performance on two camping tents

Approximatively 00:15:00










Trajectory of Wills illustrates the fact that during our various meetings we transmit values and knowledge that we share with our comrades in a continuous trade. The videos are people I have met while being in movement between Moncton, Montreal and Sackville. Most of those people don't know each other, but their images are meeting throughout this video projection.

Thanks to New Brunswick Arts Board for their financial support
