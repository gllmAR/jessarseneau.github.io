Sunday sunday



Starting October 2015, I am moving to a place to another every week and stay from Sunday to Sunday. In exchange for a couch or any other type of material that allows a night rest (mattress, yoga mat, etc.), the host receives an artwork that will be produced during that stay in her/his flat.

This page will be continually updated so that you are aware of which dates available.
