Lost Idyll

2015

Digital video

00:11:32 loop


A woman is carrying her huge and bulky scaffolding in a forest and wide fields. Real construction of temporary structures in urban landscapes, scaffolding characterizes disconcerting bustling real estate market, capitalist soil of our collective imaginary. My goal is to articulate the tension between the individual imaginary and the fictional imaginary catalyzed in everyday life.
