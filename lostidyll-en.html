Lost Idyll

2015

Digital video

00:11:32 loop


This video follows a woman who transports her enormous and cumbersome scaffolding on wheels through uncluttered fields and ploughed lands.

This gesture of metaphoric accents is repeating from a place to the other while the woman enters the field of one video projection to go out of the other. By pulling her scaffolding, she transports at the same time her individual imaginary and a collective memory that recalls to a structure which we are subjected, the construction of a post-capitalist environment, which joins in a tension between the individual, the collective and the fictitious imaginary.
