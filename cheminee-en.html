Jessica Arseneau

Maybe I Needed a chimney

2014-2015

Performance

00:20:00


At Fantaisie des Escales: Bouillon #1 (2014) with Les Escales Improbables de Montréal, Montreal QC







At Synthetic Zero Event (2015), BronxArtSpace, New York City, NY







How do we position ourselves through the spectacle provided in the everyday, and the fictitious constructions that completes it? In this performance, the Chromakey green color is used as a reference to the manufacturing of these fictitious images. The human body carries the the air vent pipes, lungs of buildings and postindustrial architectures, with which bodies are dancing in an irrational way. The acrylic sheet creates a reflection and a distortion of the projected image by recalling to the porous distance between daily information and reality.
