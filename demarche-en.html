About




My work ranges from performance to installation using digital media, objects found in constructing landscapes, household furniture or unexpected encounters during explorations. It embodies my reflections on various aspects of mobility, such as accessibility, precariousness and the collective imaginary surrounding a specific milieu.

  I am a Canadian multidisciplinary artist of Acadian origin. In 2011, I have received a B.A. in Visual Arts from Université de Moncton (New Brunswick) and I lived in Montreal (Quebec) from 2012 to 2015. I am in Germany since Summer 2015.
