Jessica Arseneau

























Stratas

2015

Installation in a wasteland

Laval, Montreal's suburb, QC


The installation is mainly conceived of venitien blinds and lumbers. The positioning of the pieces of wood forms the basic structure which, with the depth and the perspective, forms an architecture.

Moved outdoor, the venitien blinds are not anymore dividing private and public space. Here, this open construction allows the ambiguity of the dysfunctional object as well as decompartmentalizing in a figurative sense.
