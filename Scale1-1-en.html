Jessica Arseneau
::: portfolio


Situated at the entrance of Galerie Bipolar, the piece responds to the architecture of crude characteristic. The photography, thus, takes a sculptural and organic vocabulary. It addresses the relation between precarious status and the land while referring to Jorge Luis Borge’s fictive story, Del rigor en la ciencia (1944). Borges describes a map that was scaled exactly, inch per inch, to the province that it referred to, and that had become tattered over the years. Among several interpretations of this text, the envelope and the copy allegory convey that desire of absolute mastery of space and its mirroring.
