Jessica Arseneau









At Galerie Bipolar, Leipzig



Scale 1 : 1

2016

digital print on canvas and latex paint on wall

print dimensions: 132cm x 252cm


This piece refers to Jorge Luis Borge’s fictive story, Del rigor en la ciencia (1944). Borges describes a map that was scaled exactly, inch per inch, to the province that it referred to, and that had become tattered over the years. Among several interpretations of this text, the envelope and the copy allegory convey that desire of absolute mastery of space and its mirroring.

For Scale 1 : 1 , the photography takes a sculptural and organic vocabulary. The installation opens a reflection on the relation between precarious status and the land.
