Pour les transactionnaires (another nature)

2014

Shopping cart, wood, drywall, acrylic, television screen, digital video with sound and performance

36" x 48" x 52"

Loop

At the Rainbow Centre Shopping Mall, FAAS4 : Not for Sale with Galerie du Nouvel-Ontario, Sudbury ON








In the television, a man says all the words that have the prefix “trans” in the reverse order of the alphabet going down to “transaction”. Then I recite some lyrics in relation to such words like “transcoding a cityscape, transcoding a language, transcoding an action“ and also all the transactions I have made since 1 May 2014.
