It's Falling From the Sky Since a Few Days

2012

Installation, silkscreen, paper, container, fabric, umbrella structure, modeling paste

Galerie Sans Nom, Moncton NB. Produced with the Imago Boursary 2011


This was the first project after completing my bachelor degree at Université de Moncton. It was made with the Imago Bursary at Atelier d'estampe Imago.













It's Falling From the Sky Since a Few Days is the simulation of a natural disaster or rather supernatural. An ice cream cone meteorite crashed to the ground triggering torrential rain. The exhibition is the installation of objects that witness the event: Para-Ice-Cream , Meteorite Cone and Flavor Catchers. This staging in the gallery reflect to a sensory abundance in the daily generated by the consumption surounding.
